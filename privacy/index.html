Primetime Privacy Policy

Effective 2026-05-15

Primetime does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or usage data. The app runs entirely on your device, makes no network requests, and has no analytics, accounts, or third-party SDKs.

The service maintains no servers and gathers nothing from user activity. Platform-level crash reporting and usage statistics from Apple and Google fall under their own privacy frameworks rather than Primetime's policies.
